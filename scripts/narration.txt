MedFlow Pro Demo Narration
==========================

1. Welcome
"This is MedFlow Pro — a medical billing and revenue cycle management workspace. We'll walk through a complete workflow from login to claim submission and payment."

2. Login
"We sign in with our staff account. The app is scoped to one organization so all data stays within your practice."

3. Dashboard
"After login we see the dashboard: KPI cards, A/R aging, and the claims queue. Empty states are normal before adding data."

4. Patients
"We open the Patients tab and add a new patient: name, date of birth, and optional insurance ID. This builds the roster used across claims and payments."

5. Claims setup
"In Claims we first add a provider and a payer using quick add. Providers are rendering clinicians; payers are insurance plans. Both are required for drafting claims."

6. Claim draft
"We select the patient, provider, and payer. Then we add at least one procedure — CPT code, description, units, and charge — and one diagnosis with an ICD-10 code. We save the draft."

7. Submit claim
"In the claims queue we submit the draft. Status moves to submitted. The claim is now in the billing pipeline."

8. Payments
"We switch to Payments, create a new payment link for the patient, enter an amount and description, and generate the link."

9. Demo checkout
"We open the demo checkout page. This is an internal simulation — no real gateway. We click Simulate success to complete the payment."

10. Wrap-up
"The payment shows as completed and, when linked to a claim, can update claim status. That’s the core MedFlow Pro flow: patients, claims, submissions, and payments in one workspace."
